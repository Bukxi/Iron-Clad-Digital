About Us

We help startups and remote workers stay protected in a rapidly changing digital world.

Our Story

AI Shield was started after seeing how many small businesses and remote workers struggled to keep up with growing cybersecurity threats. Many companies could not afford large security teams, leaving them vulnerable to phishing scams, malware, and data breaches.

We created AI Shield to provide affordable and practical protection for people who need strong digital security without complicated systems or expensive software.

What We Stand For

Accessible Security

We believe cybersecurity tools should be simple, affordable, and available to businesses of every size.

Real-Time Protection

Our goal is to stop threats before they become major problems by using smart monitoring and rapid response systems.

Trust & Transparency
