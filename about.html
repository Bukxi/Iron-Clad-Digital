About Us

AI Shield helps startups and remote workers stay protected from modern digital threats.

Our Story

AI Shield was created after seeing how difficult cybersecurity can be for small businesses and remote professionals. Many people lacked affordable protection and struggled to defend themselves against phishing scams, malware, and online attacks.

We started this business to provide practical and reliable security solutions for people who need strong protection without complicated systems or expensive software.
